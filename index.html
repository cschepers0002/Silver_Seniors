Reclaim Your Health!

Silver Seniors is a fitness and wellness center based in Lousiville, Ky. We want all senior citizens living a healthy, active lifestyle through exercise, nutritious food, social interaction, and mental health advocacy. Silver Seniors offers multiple avenue to explore what a healthy lifestyle to you means. Let us help you today!


All Services are Free!

Silver Seniors is here to operate for your convinence, not to add finanical burden to your life. We operate from donations given to us by the community along with government provided grants. Our Silver Seniors members also donate, but this is not required.
At no cost to you, you can become a healthier you!
